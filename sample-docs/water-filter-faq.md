# Contoso PureFlow F2 Water Filter - FAQ

**Q: How often should I replace the PureFlow F2 cartridge?**
A: Every two months, or after 60 liters of water, whichever comes first. If
your tap water is harder than 200 ppm, replace the cartridge monthly.

**Q: How do I install the cartridge?**
A: Soak the new cartridge in cold water for five minutes, then push it firmly
into the socket at the bottom of the Aurora X1 water tank until it clicks.
Run one full tank of water through the machine (without coffee) before
brewing.

**Q: Does the filter remove chlorine?**
A: Yes. The F2 uses activated carbon that removes chlorine taste and odor,
plus an ion-exchange resin that reduces calcium and magnesium hardness by
about 60 percent.

**Q: Can I use the machine without a filter?**
A: Yes, the filter is optional. Without it, descale more often - every 100
liters instead of every 200.

**Q: Is the F2 compatible with other machines?**
A: The F2 fits the Contoso Aurora X1 and Aurora X2 only. The older Contoso
Classic line uses the discontinued F1 cartridge; Contoso publishes an adapter
(part number AD-11) that lets a Classic tank accept F2 cartridges.

**Q: How should I store spare cartridges?**
A: Sealed, in a cool dry place, up to 18 months from the production date
printed on the foil pouch. Do not refrigerate or freeze cartridges.
