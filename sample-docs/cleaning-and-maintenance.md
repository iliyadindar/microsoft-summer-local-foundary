# Contoso Aurora X1 - Cleaning and Maintenance Schedule

## Daily

Empty and rinse the drip tray. Purge the steam wand after each use and wipe it
with a damp cloth immediately - dried milk hardens within minutes and can
block the tip. Knock out the puck and rinse the portafilter and basket with
hot water; do not use soap on the portafilter, because soap residue clings to
the metal and taints the next shot.

## Weekly

Backflush the group head using the blind basket (the basket with no holes,
stored in the accessory drawer) and a half dose of Contoso CleanCaf detergent.
Run five 10-second cycles. Unscrew the steam wand tip and soak it in warm
water. Wash the water tank and drip tray in the top rack of a dishwasher or
by hand.

## Monthly

Remove the shower screen with a flat screwdriver and soak it in CleanCaf
solution for 20 minutes. Inspect the group head gasket for cracks; a worn
gasket shows up as water leaking around the portafilter during brewing.

## Descaling

Descale the Aurora X1 every 200 liters - the machine reminds you with error
code E03. Use only Contoso DescalePro liquid, diluted 1:10 with water. Citric
acid or vinegar are not recommended: vinegar attacks the boiler's brass
fittings and voids the warranty if damage results.

To run the descale program: empty the tank, fill it with the diluted
solution, then hold the two-cup and steam buttons together for five seconds.
The program runs for about 20 minutes, pausing halfway so you can replace the
solution with fresh water for the rinse phase. When the display shows "rin",
refill with fresh water and press the two-cup button to finish.
