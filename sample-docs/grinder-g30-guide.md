# Contoso BurrMaster G30 Grinder - Quick Guide

## Overview

The BurrMaster G30 is a conical burr grinder with 30 grind settings. Setting 1
is the finest (Turkish) and setting 30 is the coarsest (cold brew). For
espresso on the Aurora X1, start at setting 8 and adjust by one step at a
time. For drip coffee use settings 18 to 22, and for French press use 24 to
28.

## Dosing

The G30 has a built-in scale and doses by weight. Press the portafilter cradle
switch once for a single dose (9 g default) or twice for a double dose (18 g
default). You can adjust each preset in 0.5 g increments by turning the rear
dial while holding the dose button.

## Burr Care

The steel burrs should be cleaned every two weeks with the supplied brush.
Never wash the burrs with water - moisture causes rust spots on the burr
edges within days. Once a month, run a tablespoon of Contoso GrindzClean
pellets through the grinder to absorb coffee oils.

Burrs are a wear item: expect to replace them after about 500 kg of coffee,
which is roughly five years of home use. Dull burrs show up as inconsistent
grind size and longer grinding times.

## Static and Clumping

In dry climates the G30 can produce static cling. The fix is the "RDT"
technique: mist the beans with a single spray of water before grinding. A
small clump breaker is included in the box and clips inside the grounds chute.
