# Contoso Roastery - Warranty and Returns Policy

## Warranty Coverage

Every Contoso espresso machine and grinder carries a two-year limited
warranty from the date of purchase. The warranty covers manufacturing defects
in materials and workmanship, including the boiler, pump, electronics, and -
on the Aurora X1 - the thermal fuse.

The warranty does not cover damage from scale buildup when descaling records
show the machine was not descaled at the intervals in the manual, damage from
unfiltered water above 300 ppm hardness, cosmetic wear, or burr wear on
grinders (burrs are a consumable).

## Extending the Warranty

Registering your machine at contoso-roastery.example/register within 60 days
of purchase extends the warranty to three years at no cost. Registration
requires the serial number, which is printed under the drip tray.

## Making a Claim

Contact support with your proof of purchase and the machine's serial number.
For machines bought directly from Contoso, support arranges free pickup. For
machines bought from a retailer, Contoso ships a prepaid label; you provide
the box. Warranty repairs take 10 business days door to door on average.

## Returns

Unused machines in original packaging may be returned within 30 days for a
full refund. Used machines may be returned within 30 days for a refund minus
a 15 percent restocking fee, provided they are descaled and cleaned. Filter
cartridges, cleaning supplies, and coffee beans are consumables and cannot be
returned once opened.
